Zhehui Zhang a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of California, Los Angeles, advised by Prof. [Songwu Lu](http://web.cs.ucla.edu/~slu/on_research.html). She received B.Sci. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Her research interests include: mobile and wireless networks, mobile computing, network and system security.
